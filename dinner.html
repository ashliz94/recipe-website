Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 2 slices bread
  • Splash of milk
  • ½ cup ketchup (divided)
  • ½ diced onion
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Tear bread into small pieces and mix with milk to form a paste.
  3. In another bowl, combine remaining ingredients using half the ketchup.
  4. Add bread mixture and combine.
  5. Shape in a non-greased pan.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes.
  7. Spread remaining ketchup on top.
  8. Bake another 20–25 minutes.
  9. Cool 5–10 minutes before slicing.

Pizza Pockets

Ingredients

  • 1 can biscuit dough
  • 16–20 slices pepperoni
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup pizza sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Flatten each biscuit.
  3. Add pepperoni, cheese, and about 1 tsp pizza sauce.
  4. Roll into a ball and seal edges.
  5. Brush with butter and sprinkle parmesan on top.
  6. Bake 15–20 minutes until golden.
  7. Cool for 10 minutes before eating.

Salsa Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 16 oz chunky salsa
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Tortillas (optional)

Directions

  1. Place chicken, salsa, and seasonings into a crockpot.
  2. Mix until chicken is fully coated.
  3. Cook on high for 3–4 hours or low for 6–8 hours.
  4. Top with cheese or desired toppings.
  5. Serve as tacos, burritos, bowls, or wraps.
