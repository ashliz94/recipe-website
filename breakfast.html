Breakfast Bake

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices chopped bacon
  • ½ lb ground sausage
  • ½ onion (optional)
  • 3 peeled and diced potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place potatoes in a lightly greased 9×9 pan.
  3. Beat eggs and mix in bacon, sausage, onion, and seasonings.
  4. Pour mixture over potatoes.
  5. Top with shredded cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden.
  8. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.

Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla, and peanut butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  7. Cook until bubbles form (about 2 minutes).
  8. Flip and cook another minute.
  9. Remove and serve.

Biscuit Donuts

Ingredients

  • 1 can biscuit dough
  • 2–3 quarts vegetable or canola oil

Optional Toppings

  • Granulated sugar
  • Sprinkles
  • Crushed cookies

Chocolate Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2–3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp melted butter

Directions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat for 6–8 minutes (about 350°F).
  2. Cut biscuit holes or quarter them for donut holes.
  3. Fry donuts 1–2 minutes per side.
  4. Remove and place on paper towels.
  5. Let cool 5 minutes.
  6. Add desired toppings.
