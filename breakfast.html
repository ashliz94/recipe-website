Breakfast Bake

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices chopped bacon
  • ½ lb ground sausage
  • ½ onion (optional)
  • 3 peeled and diced potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Lightly grease a 9x9 pan and add the diced potatoes.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix in bacon, sausage, onion, and seasonings.
  4. Pour mixture over the potatoes.
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden.
  8. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.
