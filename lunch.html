Panko Parmesan Chicken Tenders

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken tenderloins
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • ¼ cup parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 cup panko
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ cup vegetable or canola oil

Directions

  1. Heat oil over medium for 5–6 minutes.
  2. Prepare three bowls: flour & seasonings, eggs & milk, panko & parmesan.
  3. Dredge chicken in flour, egg, then breadcrumbs.
  4. Cook in oil for 7 minutes per side.
  5. Drain on paper towel and cool for 5 minutes.

Ham and Cheese Sliders

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Hawaiian rolls
  • ½ lb sliced ham
  • 6 slices cheddar cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Slice rolls in half.
  3. Spread mayo on bottom rolls.
  4. Add ham and cheese.
  5. Top with rolls and brush with butter.
  6. Bake 8–10 minutes and cool 5 minutes.

Bacon Cheddar Egg Salad

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Sliced bread

Directions

  1. Boil eggs for 15 minutes.
  2. Place in ice bath for 5 minutes.
  3. Mash eggs in a bowl.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  5. Serve between slices of bread.
